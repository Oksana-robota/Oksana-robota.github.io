About

Ordering and delivering pizza using a telegram bot in two clicks, without additional lessons. It will be easy for client order pizza. It will help managers to quickly view the list of orders, their statuses, keep track of accounting. Waiters will be able to view current orders, put marks, which orders have already been issued / which orders have been paid, see feedback on the order. Also, where clients can win a discount on the pizza of the day.

Features

Clients

  • order pizza in several clicks;
  • monitor order status;
  • possibility to leave feedback;
  • BONUS: try luck to get a discount on pizza of the day.

Managers

  • check amount of the orders;
  • add, delete or updates menu;
  • possibility to see feedbacks.

Employees

  • monitor current open orders;
  • mark orders that have been issued or already paid;
  • check order feedbacks.
